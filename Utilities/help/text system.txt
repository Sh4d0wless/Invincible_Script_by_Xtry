You can set and edit the script's text display with 3 colors (premary,secondary & tertiary) & a logo style, it looks like a simple theme, and you can choose the colors you want.

Premary colour is for such as an important info, like nickname,address,and any other script's infos

Secondary colour is for normal script text

Tertiary colour is for logos,(,),..etc

You can see it once you try it.
